About Cru Belize

CRU IS A SOLIDARITY COMMUNITY AND PASSIONATE TO CONNECT PEOPLE WITH JESUS ​​CHRIST.

COMMUNITIES THAT TRANSFORM CITIES
Campus Crusade for Christ International (now also known as Cru) was founded by Bill and Vonette Bright in 1951 at the University of California at Los Angeles (UCLA). God led them to see the strategic value of college students to help fulfill the Great Commission. Today more than 25,000 coordinators serve with this ministry around the world. Currently the president of Campus Crusade for Christ International is Steve Douglas and the global offices are located in Orlando, Florida, USA. Today Cru is present in Belize adding to the vast growing numbers of Cru ministries around the Caribbean and the world. Cru Belize was founded in the summer of 2017 by Basilio Choc. Cru Belize has been improving and growing ever since. Cru Belize has launched its first ever college groups at the University of Belize and aiming to reach more people and students by 2020-2030. Our mission is to Win, build and send multiplying disciples centered in Christ. Faith, Growth, Abundant Fruit
